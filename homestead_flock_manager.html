Predator protection

Practical measures for your homestead

📊
Log losses under the Loss tab first — knowing which predator hits you most tells you where to invest protection effort.
🦅

Hawk protection

  • Run fishing line or wire in a grid pattern overhead — hawks won't dive through tight spaces
  • Plant dense cover bushes around the yard so birds can shelter instantly
  • Your rooster is your early-warning system — he'll alarm-call at aerial threats
  • Restrict free-range time to mid-morning and avoid early morning / dusk (peak hawk hours)
🦦

Mongoose protection

  • Use hardware cloth (not chicken wire) buried 30 cm underground around the coop perimeter
  • Lock the coop door every single night — mongooses hunt at dusk and before dawn
  • An automatic timer coop door is a worthwhile investment (₹1,500–3,000)
  • Remove any leftover feed from the yard in the evening — it draws mongooses in
🐕

Dog & cat control

  • A solid 4-foot fence keeps neighbourhood dogs out — reinforce the base with stone or buried wire
  • Young chicks are cat targets — keep them in a covered brooder until 4 weeks old minimum
  • Motion-sensor lights near the coop deter night-time approaches
