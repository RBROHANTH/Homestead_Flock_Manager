Predator protection

Practical measures for your homestead

📊Log losses first — knowing your top predator tells you where to act.
🦅

Hawk protection

  • String wire or fishing line overhead in a grid — hawks won't dive through tight gaps
  • Plant dense cover bushes for instant shelter
  • Your rooster is a live alarm system for aerial threats
  • Restrict free-range to mid-morning; avoid early dawn and dusk
🦦

Mongoose protection

  • Hardware cloth buried 30 cm underground around the full coop perimeter
  • Lock coop door every night — mongooses hunt at dusk and before dawn
  • Automatic timer coop door (₹1,500–3,000) pays for itself fast
  • Remove leftover feed from the yard each evening
🐕

Dog & cat control

  • Solid 4-foot fence with buried wire base keeps strays out
  • Keep young chicks in a covered brooder until 4 weeks minimum
  • Motion-sensor lights near the coop deter night approaches
